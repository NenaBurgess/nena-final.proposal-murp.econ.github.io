# Final Recommendation: The Sanctuary at Poplar Point

We recommend a primary capital allocation to **The Sanctuary at Poplar Point**.

### Tradeoff & Opportunity Cost Analysis
The core tradeoff exists between **short-term financial gain** in saturated markets (Project Connect and Tysons) versus **long-term value creation** in an underserved corridor.
* Project Connect and Tysons offer safer returns but reinforce existing growth reaching a point of diminishing returns.
* Poplar Point fills critical service gaps that the private market has historically failed to address.

### Executive Justification
1. **Generational Opportunity:** Despite the marginal RLV, this site represents a unique chance to capture long-term urban transformation.
2. **Market Validation:** The selection of the Therme Group for a neighboring project signals massive institutional confidence and high market demand.
3. **Equitable Growth:** By prioritizing inclusive growth and "aging in place," the project supports the 33% of local seniors living in poverty.
4. **Resilient Public Benefit:** The project utilizes recession-proof yields from medical and grocery anchors to fulfill a unique civic vision.
