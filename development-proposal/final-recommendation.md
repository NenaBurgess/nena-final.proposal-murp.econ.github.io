# Final Recommendation: The Sanctuary at Poplar Point

[cite_start]We recommend a primary capital allocation to **The Sanctuary at Poplar Point**[cite: 82].

### Tradeoff & Opportunity Cost Analysis
[cite_start]The core tradeoff exists between **short-term financial gain** in saturated markets (Project Connect and Tysons) versus **long-term value creation** in an underserved corridor[cite: 76].
* [cite_start]Project Connect and Tysons offer safer returns but reinforce existing growth reaching a point of diminishing returns[cite: 77].
* [cite_start]Poplar Point fills critical service gaps that the private market has historically failed to address[cite: 78].

### Executive Justification
1.  [cite_start]**Generational Opportunity:** Despite the marginal RLV, this site represents a unique chance to capture long-term urban transformation[cite: 83].
2.  [cite_start]**Market Validation:** The selection of the Therme Group for a neighboring project signals massive institutional confidence and high market demand[cite: 49, 84].
3.  [cite_start]**Equitable Growth:** By prioritizing inclusive growth and "aging in place," the project supports the 33% of local seniors living in poverty[cite: 70, 289].
4.  [cite_start]**Resilient Public Benefit:** The project utilizes recession-proof yields from medical and grocery anchors to fulfill a unique civic vision[cite: 64, 304].
